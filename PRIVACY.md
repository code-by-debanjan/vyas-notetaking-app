# Privacy Policy for Vyas

_Last updated: March 9, 2026_

## Overview

Vyas ("the App") is a desktop note-taking application developed by code-by-debanjan. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Collection

Vyas does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely offline on your local device.

## File Access

The App accesses files on your device only when you explicitly open, create, or save files through the App's interface. All file operations are performed locally. No file content is uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

## Font Access

The App reads the list of fonts installed on your system solely to provide font selection functionality within the editor. This information is not stored or transmitted.

## Analytics and Telemetry

The App does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking mechanisms.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not connect to any third-party services, APIs, or servers.

## Data Storage

All application preferences and data are stored locally on your device. No cloud storage or synchronization is used.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/code-by-debanjan/vyasa-notetaking-app).
